Here are the predictions made this week:

From Paleofuture: “Predictions for the Year 2018 From the 20th Century.”

“Some 2018 Predictions” from Washington State University’s Mike Caulfield.

Via eSchool News: “25 education trends for 2018.”

Folks really really really want AR and VR to be a thing – this year as in the past. According to MIT Technology Review, these are “Five of the Coolest-Looking VR and AR Headsets, Apps, and More Coming in 2018.”

Via Futurism: “California Is Officially Planning to Ban Polluting Cars by 2040.”
