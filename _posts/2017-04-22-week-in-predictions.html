As part of this new Contrafabulists' project, we will be tracking on predictions that technologists make each week...

Facebook predicts that “over the next 2 years, we will be building systems that demonstrate the capability to type at 100 wpm by decoding neural activity devoted to speech.”

Elon Musk predicts that “within eight to 10 years healthy people could be getting brain implants as new computer interfaces.”

The Institute for the Future says that humans cannot think farther beyond a five year horizon.
