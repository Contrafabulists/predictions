As part of a new Contrafabulists’ project, we are monitoring predictions that technologists and marketers make each week…

“Augmented Reality Software Market expected to reach USD 35.22 billion by 2022,” according to a press release from Markets and Markets.

“Adoption of AR and VR Technology Poised to Grow Rapidly between 2017 and 2025,” according to a press release from Future Market Insights.

“Virtual digital assistants will overtake world population by 2021,” says ZDNet.

According to Florida Today, CSU researcher Phil Klotzbach predicts that hurricane projections for 2017 may increase. His project, the Topical Meterology Project, predicts “11 named storms would form this year. Four of these storms would grow into hurricanes, two of which would strengthen into major hurricanes of Category 3, 4 or 5.”
